Awesome Features

Weather Details

With a stunning peaceful layout, daily weather details include: sunrise, sunset times, precipitation probablity, summary and more.

Moonlight

Weekly report of the fraction of the moon illuminated by direct sunlight. There are 8 traditionally recognized phases and handled in the app. But, a more refined description is the percent of the moon's surface illuminated.

New/Full Moon Phases

Display of a full year of upcoming moon phases. By anticipating and having clarity about these significant dates, it can assist in schedulling activities and understanding cycles. Plus, it is just cool to know.
